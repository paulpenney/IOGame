          Tips

          • Your function must be named buildCharacter.
          • It must return an object with these fields: characterName, color, size, speed, maxHealth, powers.
          • Each power has a name, a key to press, a cooldownMs, and a cast object.
          • cast.kind is one of: projectile, area, melee, dash, shield, or heal.
          • See the Power Manual for every field and its allowed range.
          • Press WASD or arrow keys to move; Shift sprints, C dodge-rolls, your power's key fires.
